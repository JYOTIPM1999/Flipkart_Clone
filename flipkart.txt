	a. 2 types of bar.

2  Sign up - signup through email id, Otp, JWT auth, Oauth, RBCA.

3. Log in - Login through OTP or password. Reset password. Don't have account signup 1st.

4. Navbar dropdown- Profile, My order, wishlist, logout(logged user out from the site).

5. Profile- 
	a. my order same as My order on the navbar.
	b. Account info- profile info, Manage Addresses, PAN card.
	c. Payment info- save UPI, save cards.
	d. My wishlist same as the navbar wishlist.
	e. Logout.

6. Order- Filter, order details related to the user.

7. Cart- Product Image, Product name, product details, amount, delivery date, you can't increase qty more than 5, save for later, remove the product, and place the order, price details on the right side of the cart page.

8. Place an order - Login, deliver details, order summary, and payment option.

9. Order details- Delivery address, product details.

10. Customer service chat app- Select the product for raising a query.

11. Product page- Sorting, pagination, grid.

12. Single product page- Gallary, Breadcrumbs, Product details, Add to cart, Buy now, Product desc, Specification, ratings & review(star, images, desc), Q&A(like, dislike comments), recommendation similar product.

13. Payment gateway.
